Help

1

Here you can search for a specific Pokémon. You can search for a Pokémon with its ID or name. You need the whole correct name to find a Pokémon. Rather click on the magnifying glass or press enter to confirm your search. When you want to see more Pokémon in the list. Scroll down until you hit the button of the screen and more Pokémon will be loaded

2

Click on the Pokémon to get more information about it.

3

You can use the control panel to switch throw the Pokémon in the list using the left and right button. When you want further information about the chosen Pokémon press the upper or lower button.

4

For closing the detailed Pokémon information screen click on the yellow triangle on the right side
